#### R-002: Staff Capacity Constraints
- **Phase:** Phase 2-4
- **Impact:** High
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Description:** Existing staff unable to deliver new programmes alongside current workload
- **Mitigation:**
  - Early hiring decisions (Phase 1)
  - Phased rollout to manage load
  - Workload monitoring and adjustment
  - Temporary/contract staff for peaks
  - Prioritise high-margin activities
- **Owner:** CEO / Clinical Manager
- **Status:** Open
- **Review Date:** Monthly

#### R-003: Medical-Aid Claim Delays/Rejections
- **Phase:** Phase 2 onwards
- **Impact:** High
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Description:** Cash flow strain from slow payment or rejected claims
- **Mitigation:**
  - Pre-verify benefits before admission/enrolment
  - Submit complete documentation
  - Daily pre-auth tracking
  - Panel expansion (Phase 4)
  - Deposit collection protocol
- **Owner:** Admin Coordinator
- **Status:** Open
- **Review Date:** Weekly

#### R-004: Quality Compromised by Growth
- **Phase:** Phase 3-4
- **Impact:** High
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Description:** Rapid scaling leads to quality lapses, complaints, or compliance breaches
- **Mitigation:**
  - Clinical supervision structures
  - Regular feedback loops
  - Quality audits (Phase 4)
  - Incident monitoring
  - Staff training and competency checks
- **Owner:** Clinical Manager
- **Status:** Open
- **Review Date:** Monthly

#### R-005: Compliance Issues Discovered
- **Phase:** Phase 1 onwards
- **Impact:** High
- **Likelihood:** Low
- **Description:** Regulatory gaps or licence issues halt service delivery

  - Stay current on regulatory changes

- **Owner:** Clinical Admin
- **Status:** Open
- **Review Date:** Quarterly

## Escalation Protocol

1. **Green risks:** Monitor per schedule; report in monthly summary
2. **Yellow risks:** Discuss at weekly project meetings; document mitigation progress
3. **Red risks:** Immediate escalation to steering committee; daily monitoring

---

## Risk Closure Criteria

Risks may be closed when:
- Mitigation fully implemented and effective
- Risk event has passed without occurring
- Risk transferred or accepted by senior management
- Project phase where risk applies has completed
