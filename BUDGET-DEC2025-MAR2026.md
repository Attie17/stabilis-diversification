## RISK FACTORS & MITIGATION

### High-Priority Risks

#### 1. Cash Flow Crisis (CRITICAL)
- **Risk:** Insufficient reserves to cover R1.99M deficit
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** Severe (business closure)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Secure R350k credit facility before Dec 1
  - Daily cash monitoring (13-week rolling forecast)
  - Accelerated AR collections (targeting R478k)
  - Defer non-essential capex to Q2 2026

#### 2. Wellness Centre Delayed Launch
- **Risk:** Renovations take longer than 4 weeks
- **Probability:** Medium-High
- **Impact:** High (R262k revenue at risk)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Fixed-price contract with penalty clauses
  - Weekly progress reviews with contractor
  - Backup temporary space identified
  - Phased launch option (start with existing rooms)

#### 3. Revenue Shortfall (60% Not Achieved)
- **Risk:** Actual revenue <60% of targets
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** High (additional R150-200k deficit)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Conservative projections already (60% not 100%)
  - Marketing budget front-loaded
  - Referral network activation in Dec
  - Pricing incentives for early adopters

#### 4. Staff Turnover During Crisis
- **Risk:** Key clinical staff leave during turnaround
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** Severe (service delivery disruption)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Transparent communication about recovery plan
  - Retention bonuses for critical staff
  - Flexible working arrangements
  - Monthly staff feedback sessions

#### 5. Regulatory/Compliance Issues
- **Risk:** DOH/SARS shuts down operations
- **Probability:** Low-Medium
- **Impact:** Catastrophic
- **Mitigation:**
  - SARS payment plan signed by Dec 10
  - Monthly compliance audits
  - Legal counsel on retainer
  - Pre-emptive engagement with regulators
