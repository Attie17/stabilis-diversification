# Phase 1 – Mobilisation
**Timeline:** 17 Nov – 15 Dec 2025  
**Timezone:** Africa/Johannesburg

## Phase Outcome
Prepared operational and administrative base; roles clear; pricing finalised; compliance cleared; rollout calendar approved.

---

## Milestones

### P1-M1: Kick-off Meeting
- **Owner:** CEO
- **Due:** 17 Nov 2025
- **Status:** ⏳ PLANNED

**Description:**  
Convene all staff involved; present objectives, timelines, expectations; confirm comms cadence.

**Expected Outcome:**  
Shared understanding of goals, scope, pace; explicit commitments per function.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Schedule meeting with all key staff
- [ ] Prepare presentation deck (objectives, timelines, expectations)
- [ ] Define communication cadence (weekly/bi-weekly)
- [ ] Document explicit commitments by function
- [ ] Distribute meeting minutes within 24 hours

---

### P1-M2: Appoint Leads
- **Owner:** CEO
- **Due:** 20 Nov 2025
- **Status:** ⏳ PLANNED

**Description:**  
Appoint Adult Clinical Lead, Youth Lead, Finance Officer, Admin Coordinator, Comms Officer; issue written briefs.

**Expected Outcome:**  
Accountability matrix with named owners and escalation path.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Appoint Adult Clinical Lead
- [ ] Appoint Youth Lead
- [ ] Appoint Finance Officer
- [ ] Appoint Admin Coordinator
- [ ] Appoint Comms Officer
- [ ] Issue written role briefs to each lead
- [ ] Create accountability matrix with escalation paths
- [ ] Get written acceptance from each lead

---

### P1-M3: Operational Mapping
- **Owner:** Clinical Manager
- **Due:** 25 Nov 2025
- **Status:** ⏳ PLANNED

**Description:**  
Map rooms, rosters, group slots, referral flow, bed capacity, bottlenecks.

**Expected Outcome:**  
Baseline capacity map and constraints list for scheduling.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Map all treatment rooms and availability
- [ ] Document current staff rosters
- [ ] Identify available group therapy slots
- [ ] Chart referral flow from enquiry to admission
- [ ] Calculate bed capacity and current utilisation
- [ ] Identify operational bottlenecks
- [ ] Create visual capacity map
- [ ] Generate constraints list for scheduling team

---

### P1-M4: Pricing & Billing Policy
- **Owner:** Finance & Admin
- **Due:** 28 Nov 2025
- **Status:** ⏳ PLANNED

**Description:**  
Confirm tariffs for inpatient, adult OP, adolescent OP, aftercare; embed deposit and pre-auth rules; draft admissions SOP.

**Expected Outcome:**  
Board-approved pricing schedule + admissions policy ready.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Confirm inpatient tariffs
- [ ] Set adult outpatient programme pricing
- [ ] Set adolescent outpatient programme pricing
- [ ] Define aftercare subscription model pricing
- [ ] Document deposit requirements by service line
- [ ] Define pre-authorisation rules
- [ ] Draft admissions SOP with billing workflow
- [ ] Present to board for approval
- [ ] Obtain board sign-off on pricing schedule
- [ ] Publish approved pricing internally

---

### P1-M5: Compliance Check
- **Owner:** Clinical Admin
- **Due:** 08 Dec 2025
- **Status:** ⏳ PLANNED

**Description:**  
Verify registrations/licences; confirm medical-aid panel docs; list/close gaps.

**Expected Outcome:**  
Compliance status green; documentation pack complete.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Verify facility registration current
- [ ] Verify all clinical licences current
- [ ] Confirm medical-aid panel membership status
- [ ] Review panel documentation completeness
- [ ] List all compliance gaps
- [ ] Create action plan to close gaps
- [ ] Close all identified gaps
- [ ] Assemble complete documentation pack
- [ ] Get sign-off from compliance officer

---

### P1-M6: Finalize Implementation Calendar
- **Owner:** Project Manager
- **Due:** 15 Dec 2025
- **Status:** ⏳ PLANNED

**Description:**  
Publish 15-month calendar covering Phases 2–5; lock milestone dates.

**Expected Outcome:**  
Signed-off master schedule for 2026 rollout.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Draft 15-month calendar (Jan 2026 – Mar 2027)
- [ ] Include all Phase 2–5 milestones
- [ ] Mark critical dependencies
- [ ] Identify resource constraints
- [ ] Review with all phase leads
- [ ] Incorporate feedback

- [ ] Obtain executive sign-off

---

## Phase 1 Success Criteria
- ✅ All 6 milestones completed on time
- ✅ Accountability matrix with named owners established
- ✅ Board-approved pricing and admissions policy in place
- ✅ Compliance documentation complete and current
- ✅ Master implementation calendar signed off
- ✅ All staff aligned on objectives and timeline

## Risk Register (Phase 1)

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|--------|------------|------------|-------|
| Key staff unavailable for kick-off | High | Low | Schedule 2 weeks ahead; confirm attendance | CEO |
| Compliance gaps discovered late | High | Medium | Start compliance check early; weekly status | Clinical Admin |
| Board delays pricing approval | Medium | Medium | Present draft early; build consensus pre-meeting | Finance |
| Leads reject appointment | High | Low | Pre-consult candidates; clarify scope & support | CEO |
